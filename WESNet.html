Tongji Surround-view Dataset

dataset.zip (password: wapi)

We collected our own dataset by an open-topped electric car equipped with four cameras mounted around on campus, which mainly consists of two parts, calibrated images and natural ones. The calibrated images were collected over the calibration site to provide weak supervision information, while the natural ones, which act as training and testing sets, were taken from natural scenes. The original resolutions of all collected images are 1080p. Typical image samples contained in this dataset is shown below.

This original dataset contains 19,078 groups of fisheye images captured synchronously by SVS. In our implementation, the extrinsics' settings are augmented into nine different types, and the dataset containing about 170,000 groups of images is established. The 10% of the data is splited to the testing set (17,000 groups), and the rest forms the training set (155,000 groups).
